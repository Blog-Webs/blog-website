# Tech Blogs

The **Tech Blogs** module is your primary source for high-quality, long-form content. Whether you're looking for deep dives into software engineering concepts, tutorials, or opinion pieces on the latest AI trends, you'll find them here, written by industry professionals and community experts.

Here is an overview of how to navigate and engage with the Tech Blogs.

---

## 📖 1. Curated Articles & Reading Experience
We've spent a significant amount of time optimizing the reading experience so you can focus entirely on the content.

* **Dynamic Reading Layout:** When you open an article, you are presented with a clean, distraction-free UI featuring beautiful typography.
* **Sticky Table of Contents:** On the left side of the screen, a dynamic Table of Contents tracks your position in the article, allowing you to instantly jump between sections.
* **Reading Progress Bar:** A sleek progress bar at the top of the screen visually indicates how far along you are in the article.

---

## 🔖 2. Bookmarking (Read Later)
Found an incredible article but don't have time to read a 15-minute deep dive right now?

* **Save for Later:** Simply click the **Bookmark** icon at the top of the article.
* **Personal Reading List:** Bookmarked articles are immediately saved to your personal dashboard and your StudentOS profile. You can access your Reading List at any time and pick up exactly where you left off.

---

## 🗣️ 3. Engagement & Discussions
Reading is just the first step. The real value comes from discussing the concepts with the author and the community.

* **Likes & Appreciation:** If an article helped you solve a problem or learn something new, click the Heart icon to show the author some appreciation!
* **Nested Comments:** Scroll down to the bottom of the article to view the comment section. You can leave top-level comments or reply directly to other users to start threaded discussions.
* **Rich Profiles:** Click on the author's avatar or name at the top of the article to view their full profile, see their other published works, and follow them for future updates.

---
